About Me

Hi! I’m Junior Langie, and I’m currently a student who enjoys building websites and bringing ideas to life through design and code. I’m still learning and improving every day, but I really enjoy the process, especially turning something that looks plain into something that looks clean and feels nice to use.

I’m comfortable working with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I’ve been exploring areas like UI/UX design, responsive layouts, and basic front-end interactivity. I like projects that involve real people or real businesses, because it helps me think about what users actually need instead of just what looks cool.

Outside of coding, I love experimenting with design styles, reading, and discovering new tools or techniques that can help me build better websites. I’m always looking for opportunities to grow, learn, and collaborate, and hopefully create things I can be proud of.
